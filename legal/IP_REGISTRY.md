## Trade Secrets

### Classification System

**Level 1 (Crown Jewels)** - Highest protection, limited access:
- Neural network training datasets
- Neural network model weights
- Holographic compression implementation details

**Level 2 (High Value)** - Significant protection, team access:
- Parameter selection heuristics
- Optimization techniques
- Internal algorithms

**Level 3 (Confidential)** - Standard protection, documented:
- Business plans
- Customer data
- Financial information

1. **Physical Security**:
   - Air-gapped systems for Level 1 secrets

   - Secure data centers

   - Multi-factor authentication

## IP Enforcement

### Enforcement Strategy

1. **Monitor**: Regular searches for infringement
2. **Cease & Desist**: Initial warning letter
3. **Licensing**: Offer licensing before litigation
4. **Litigation**: Last resort, only for clear infringement

### Defense Strategy

1. **Prior Art**: Document all innovations thoroughly
2. **Patent Pools**: Join defensive patent pools (OIN, COPA)
3. **Insurance**: Patent defense insurance ($1-5M coverage)
4. **Legal Fund**: $5M reserved for patent defense
