## Legal Processes

### Patent Disclosure Process

**When to Disclose**:
- Novel algorithm or method
- Significant performance optimization
- Unique technical solution
- New architecture or data structure

**How to Disclose**:
1. Use [Patent Disclosure Template](templates/PATENT_DISCLOSURE_TEMPLATE.md)
2. Fill out all sections thoroughly
3. Email to: patents@nexuszero.io
4. Receive acknowledgment within 2 days
5. Review meeting within 2-4 weeks

**What Happens Next**:
- Patent committee reviews disclosure
- Decides: File patent, trade secret, or public domain
- If filing: Patent attorney prepares application
- If trade secret: Implement protection measures

### Innovation Logging

**All significant innovations must be logged** in [INNOVATION_LOG.md](INNOVATION_LOG.md).

**Why?**
- Track patent opportunities
- Document prior art
- Support patent applications
- Establish invention dates

**How?**
- Add entry to INNOVATION_LOG.md
- Include: date, inventors, description, location
- Update status as patents progress

## FAQ

### Can I use NexusZero Protocol commercially?

**Yes!** The MIT License explicitly allows commercial use without fees or royalties. Just include the license and copyright notice in your distributions.

### Do I need to share my modifications?

**No.** The MIT License does not require you to share modifications. However, we encourage contributing improvements back to benefit the community.

### How do I report a patent-worthy invention?

Use the [Patent Disclosure Template](templates/PATENT_DISCLOSURE_TEMPLATE.md) and email to patents@nexuszero.io. You'll receive a response within 2 business days.

### What if I accidentally access a trade secret?

If you gain unauthorized access to a trade secret:
1. **Stop immediately** - Don't read or copy
2. **Report** - Email security@nexuszero.io immediately
3. **Don't disclose** - Don't tell anyone else
4. **Cooperate** - Cooperate with investigation

### Can I fork NexusZero and create a competitor?

**Yes**, but:
- You cannot use our trademarks (NexusZero™)
- You must include our copyright notice and MIT License
- Our patents still apply (consider licensing for patented features)
- Trade secrets remain protected

### How do I request trademark permission?

Email trademarks@nexuszero.io with:
- Intended use
- Context (product, website, etc.)
- Commercial or non-commercial
- Duration

Response typically within 5 business days.
